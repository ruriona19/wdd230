Know this friendly and warm city

Demographic

Cochabamba is a Bolivian city, the capital of the province of Cercado and the department of the same name. It is located in the center of the country, in the central valley of the department. It has an estimated population of 856,198 inhabitants by 2022, and 1.5 million in its metropolitan area,4 also called the Kanata Metropolitan Region, (RMK) to which it belongs, along with the municipalities of: Sacaba, Quillacollo, Colcapirhua, Tiquipaya , Vinto and Sipe Sipe.

Cochabamba is located in a valley of fertile and productive land at an altitude of 2,558 m. The city, surrounded by fields of crops and valuable pre-Inca and Inca vestiges, retains its viceregal personality while becoming an important agricultural, manufacturing, commercial and industrial center. It is considered one of the three main cities in Bolivia,5 forming part of the so-called trunk axis, along with the cities of Santa Cruz de la Sierra and Nuestra Se√±ora de La Paz.
