Site Purpose

  • Group industrial, commercial and service companies, formally constituted, that carry out activities in the department of Cochabamba, organizing and keeping the corresponding registry updated.
  • Represent and defend its associates in matters related to their business activities and when it concerns the general interest of the sector.
  • Provide information, training, services and advice to Associates, thus contributing to their strengthening.
  • Promote business activity in the areas of industry, commerce and services, defending and promoting legal certainty, private property, free enterprise, the rule of law and the formalization of the economy.
  • Propose and promote actions and initiatives for the sustainable development of Cochabamba and its Associates, through the generation of an adequate investment climate, competitiveness and incentive for industrial, commercial and service activities in the Department.
  • Foster the generation of formal employment and encourage coordination in the relations between employers and workers of associated companies in the industrial, commercial and service sectors.
