Audience

Who: Our objective is all sole proprietorships, commercial companies and anyone who is interested in being part of our group and doing business in Cochabamba.

Age: Anyone over the age of 21.

Education: Any person or group of people who have graduated from high school and have decided to start a company.

Income: Above $50,000 anually.

Motivation: Be willing to learn everything related to business activity in the areas of industry, commerce and services, defending and promoting legal certainty, private property, free enterprise, the rule of law and the formalization of the economy.

Goals and motivation for using the site: Wants to promote his business in the city.

Scenarios

  • The user seeks information, training, services, and advice in order to strengthen his business venture.
  • The user seeks to promote actions and initiatives for the sustainable development of Cochabamba, through the generation of an adequate investment climate, competitiveness, and incentive for industrial, commercial, and service activities in the Department.
  • Seek training to improve their business and citizen leadership, to generate positive changes for the Department.
  • Obtain statistical information regarding business activity and the economic and social reality in Cochabamba.
  • Seek help and information to improve the capacity to generate more formal employment and encourage agreement in the relations between employers and workers.
